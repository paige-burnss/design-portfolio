Hey there, I’m Paige! I’m a third year student at the University of Colorado Boulder studying computer science and marketing. This past summer I was a User Experience Intern for Cloud Campaign!

Why Product Design?
Product design is the perfect combination of art, business, and engineering. It has given me opportunities to find creative ways to help others and solve problems. I love brainstorming hundreds of solutions to a problem in order to find the perfect fit: the square peg to fill the square hole. And the best part is, the job is never over! There's always more to learn, enhance, and change in future iterations.
