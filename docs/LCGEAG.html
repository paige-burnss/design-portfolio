Green Consulting Group: Website Design


I am a Senior Analyst in the Leeds Consulting Group (LCG) at CU Boulder. I’ve worked on several engagements with this group, consulting local companies on problems their businesses are facing and possible solutions they could implement.

LCG also has affinity groups, or groups that focus on a specific interest area of some of the analysts. The Green Consulting Group was formed a few years ago, and they have been compiling resources to send to companies to make changes to be more sustainable. I am also passionate about sustainability (see Side Projects), and I worked with this group to develop a website layout that would allow these companies to easily navigate through the website and learn about sustainability in the workplace.

ROLE:
UI Designer
TOOLS USED:
Canva, Figma
TIMELINE:
July 1st - August 10th
